Wildmutt

One of the aliens Ben possesses. As the name implies Wildmutt is an alien with a beast-like appearance further accentuated by its orange fur. Wildmutt doesn't have eyes but it makes up for it with it's amazing sense of smell and hearing. Wildmutt also have sharp claws and sharp teeth.


Wildmutt first appeared in "And then there were ten" were he destroyed on of vulgax's mechadroid.

Powers & Abilities
Type Of Ability Description
Sense of smell Even without his eyes Wildmutt can still accurately percieve his surrounding with the help of his sense of smell which acts similar to that of a thermograph
Sense of hearing Wildmutt also has an amazing sense of hearing and combined with his sense of smell they take the form of a radar or sonar. Wildmutt can track almost anything and anyone by their scent.
Teeth and claws Wildmutt possesses razor-sharp teeth and claws, the latter of which he can use to climb vertical surfaces.
Enhanced Physical Attributes Due to his species, Wildmutt also has enhanced speed, durability, reflexes, and endurance
Enhance Agility Wildmutt has enhanced agility that allows him to run, jump, and climb at an inhuman rate. He is highly acrobatic and gymnastic, with such movements being similar to that of an ape.
Quills In his adulthood, Wildmutt gains quills in his back which he can use to protect himself or launch as projectiles

Appearances throughout main renditions

While under the effects of a cold in Side Effects, Wildmutt's fur discolors from its vivid orange to a pale yellowish-orange tone, and his lips and claws turn from black to a sickly dark green hue.

In Ultimate Alien, 16-year-old Wildmutt looks mostly the same except his skin is lighter in color. While his lips were still black in the Ultimate Alien intro, they were in the same color as his fur in the actual series. He wore the Ultimatrix symbol on his chest. Kevin as Wildmutt shares these traits.

In Omniverse, Wildmutt's lips have returned to being black and his forearms are a bit bigger. 16-year-old Wildmutt wears a green and white collar with the Omnitrix symbol.

Weaknesses
Weakness Description
Sense of smell Due to him relying a lot on his sense of smell, Wildmutt was incapacitated when his sense of smell was blocked by peanut butter.
Communication Wildmutt can't speak in the human language, instead he uses a combination of barking and snarling to communicate. Due to this it is very hard for others to understand him.
Sense of hearing Wildmutt's higher hearing range can be taken advantage of, as high frequency noises like a Sonic Pitch Whistle or a Zaroffian's whistle can overwhelm his senses and stun him.
