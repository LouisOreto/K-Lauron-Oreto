Heatblast

One of the aliens Ben possesses. He has a red, molten and rocky body and fiery head with a mask-like face. He radiates high amounts of heat, has oval-like feet with only two toes and a backtoe each, and he sports a rather volcanic look. He uses fire powers, hence the name Heatblast.


Heatblast was the first alien that Ben transformed into as seen during the first episode the moment he puts the Omnitrix on, who proceeds to eventually burn the forest down upon trying the alien's powers. Below here is alterations to his powers and attacks of fire.

Powers & Abilities
Type Of Ability Description
Pyrokinetics the capability to generate and control fire at will, into any shape or form
Explosive Fire Heatblast can make his flames explode, which is helpful to generate destructive power, and to an extention make his flames mimic physical weapons
Heat Amplification He can cause his flames to highten its heat and intensity, thus enabling Ben to burn through stronger objects
Enhanced Physical Attributes As an alien, heatblast also has enhanced speed, durability, and endurance
Terrakinesis He is also capable of controlling and levitating masses of stone, rock or ground
Inverted Powers Undergoing a cold, his powers get inverted from hot fiery blasts to frosty ice blasts. The flames on his body also shifts the same way

Appearances throughout main renditions

When under the effects of a cold in Side Effects, Heatblast's fiery skin (excluding his rocky body) turned blue.

In Ultimate Alien, Heatblast closely resembled Alan, only taller, not as skinny, slightly altered and his face shape was more similar to the Original Series. His eyes were no longer connected to the fire on his head. The rocks over his body changed, with a burnt black color and red markings on every edge of his rocky skin, resembling charcoal and his plasma body is a lighter shade of yellow.

In Omniverse, Heatblast looks the same as he did in Ultimate Alien, only his eyes are once again connected to the fire on his head, like in the Original Series. He is taller and more muscular, his face's design is different and his shoulder plates are slightly tilted up. His feet are also redesigned.

Weaknesses
Type Of Weakness Description
Fire-resistant objects As his attacks are purely of fire, Heatblast's attacks have no effect against fire-resistant and diamond-like materials
Flammable Objects As Heatblast is made of fire, he can accidentally burn things down when in contact with them and leave markings such as scorch marks or footprins
Aquatic vulnerability Being exposed to water or ice for too long can extinguish his flames and immobilize him.
