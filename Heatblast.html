Heatblast

One of the aliens Ben possesses. He has a red, molten and rocky body and fiery head with a mask-like face. He uses fire powers, hence the name Heatblast.


Heatblast was the first alien that Ben transformed into as seen during the first episode the moment he puts the Omnitrix on, who proceeds to eventually burn the forest down upon trying the alien's powers. Below here is alterations to his powers and attacks of fire.

Powers & Abilities
Type Of Ability Description
Pyrokinetics the capability to generate and control fire at will, into any shape or form
Explosive Fire Heatblast can make his flames explode, which is helpful to generate destructive power, and to an extention make his flames mimic physical weapons
Heat Amplification He can cause his flames to highten its heat and intensity, thus enabling Ben to burn through stronger objects
Enhanced Physical Attributes As an alien, heatblast also has enhanced speed, durability, and endurance
Terrakinesis He is also capable of controlling and levitating masses of stone, rock or ground
Inverted Powers Undergoing a cold, his powers get inverted from hot fiery blasts to frosty ice blasts. The flames on his body also shifts the same way
